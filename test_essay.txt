Technology is something that exists in the world today and many people are using it. Technology has changed things a lot and also sometimes it does not change things depending on the situation. There are many types of technology like phones, computers, internet, and other stuff that people use everyday for doing things.
First of all technology is good because it helps people do things faster. For example if someone wants to talk to someone they can send a message or something like that. In the past people had to wait longer but now it is fast. But also sometimes it is not fast because internet might not work or battery dies which happens sometimes.
Another thing is that technology makes people smarter maybe because they can search information online. But also it makes people less smart because they depend on Google for everything and they don't remember things anymore. So technology is both making people smarter and also less smarter which is confusing but it happens.
Also technology is used in schools and offices and other places where work is done. Students use laptops for studying but sometimes they watch videos or social media instead which means technology can distract them from studying. Teachers also use technology but sometimes projectors don't work and then class becomes slow.
In conclusion technology is very important and it affects the world in many ways both positive and negative and sometimes neutral. Therefore technology is something that people should use but also not overuse because it can be helpful but also not helpful depending on the time and situation.
